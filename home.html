Who We Are

We are Finance Club, IIT Bombay—a vibrant community of finance enthusiasts passionate about promoting financial literacy and innovation on campus. Since our inception, we've been fostering a culture of excellence through workshops, competitions, and guest lectures. Our members collaborate on projects, engage in discussions, and share insights through our blogs. With the support of alumni, we're venturing into the Indian equity markets. Join us as we shape the future of finance at IIT Bombay and beyond.

What We Do

At Finance Club, IIT Bombay, we host workshops, competitions, and guest lectures to promote financial literacy and innovation.
